How to play Ô Ăn Quan

  1. Each small pit starts with 5 citizen stones; each big Quan pit holds 1 quan stone (worth 10).
  2. On your turn, click one of your five pits that has stones, then pick a direction.
  3. Stones are sown one-by-one into following pits around the board.
  4. If the pit after your last stone has stones, you scoop it up and keep sowing.
  5. If that pit is empty and the next one has stones, you capture those stones (and any beyond, while the empty/full pattern continues). Capturing a Quan pit scores 10.
  6. If two empties appear in a row, your turn ends.
  7. If all your pits are empty at the start of your turn, you spend 5 points to refill them. If you can't, you lose.
  8. When both Quan stones are captured, the game ends. Final score = captured points + stones left on your side. Highest wins.
