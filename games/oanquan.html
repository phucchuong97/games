How to play

  1. Each small pit starts with 5 stones; each big Quan pit holds 1 quan stone (worth 10).
  2. On your turn, click one of your five pits that has stones, then pick a direction.
  3. Stones are sown one-by-one into the following pits.
  4. If the pit after your last stone has stones, you scoop it and keep sowing.
  5. If that pit is empty and the next has stones, you capture them (and continue while the empty/full pattern repeats). Capturing a Quan scores 10.
  6. Two empty pits in a row end your turn.
  7. If all your pits are empty at the start of your turn, spend 5 points to refill them — if you can't, you lose.
  8. When both Quan stones are captured, the game ends. Final score = captured + stones left on your side.
